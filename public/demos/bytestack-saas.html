FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long is the free trial?
Our free trial lasts 14 days with full access to all Professional plan features. No credit card is required to start, and you can downgrade to the Starter plan or cancel at any time.
Can I switch plans at any time?
Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you will be charged a prorated amount. When downgrading, the change takes effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
Is my data secure on your platform?
Security is our top priority. We are SOC2 Type II certified, GDPR compliant, and use AES-256 encryption for all data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit. We conduct regular third-party security audits.
Do you offer on-premise deployment?
Yes, our Enterprise plan includes the option for on-premise or private cloud deployment. This is ideal for organizations with strict data residency requirements. Contact our sales team for details.
What kind of support do you provide?
Starter plans include email support with a 24-hour response time. Professional plans get priority support with a 4-hour response time. Enterprise customers receive a dedicated account manager and 24/7 phone support.
