Our Story

A Culinary Journey Through France

Founded by Chef Laurent Dubois, Savoria brings together the finest traditions of French gastronomy with contemporary innovation. Each plate is a canvas, each ingredient a brushstroke.

Our kitchen sources exclusively from local organic farms within a 50-kilometre radius, ensuring that every dish celebrates the terroir of our region while minimising our environmental footprint.
